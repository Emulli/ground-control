Mission

To provide a safe, chemical-free, and sustainable deodorizing solution by transforming recycled coffee grounds into an eco-friendly product that promotes healthier and cleaner living spaces.

02

Vision

To become a leading natural deodorizer brand that supports zero-waste living and inspires communities to choose sustainable alternatives for everyday needs.

Problem & Solution

Many deodorizing products contain harmful chemicals that can affect human health, pets, and the environment. At the same time, large amounts of used coffee grounds are wasted daily, contributing to environmental pollution.

Ground Control upcycles used coffee grounds into a natural, chemical-free deodorizer that effectively neutralizes odors while reducing waste and supporting sustainable consumption.

Technical Specifications

Project: Ground Control

Target Sectors

Ground Control is engineered for small enclosed biomes. Effective in residential closets, shoe racks, automotive interiors, sanitary spaces (bathrooms), and office storage units.

Operational Lifespan

Each unit maintains peak efficacy for 2–4 weeks depending on atmospheric density and odor intensity.

Mechanism of Action

Utilizes upcycled roasted coffee grounds to absorb volatile organic compounds (VOCs). Zero chemical emissions. 100% safe for biological entities (Humans & Pets).

Deployment Protocol

1. Remove from seal.
2. Place in target quadrant.
3. Allow passive absorption.
4. Refresh unit after effective period expires.
